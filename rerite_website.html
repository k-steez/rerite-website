About ReRite UG

ReRite is the University of Ghana's premier intellectual discourse club, dedicated to fostering meaningful conversations and critical thinking among students.

Through our weekly meetings, we create a space where ideas are exchanged freely, perspectives are challenged, and intellectual growth is nurtured.

Our Mission

To cultivate a vibrant intellectual community at the University of Ghana where students can engage in thoughtful discourse, develop critical thinking skills, and build lasting connections.
